# Future Product Boundaries

AuthToolkit Loyalty Passes should stay separate from future AuthToolkit payment products.

## AuthToolkit Loyalty Passes

- loyalty cards
- digital passes
- VIP status
- rewards
- offers
- memberships
- customer retention

## Future AuthToolkit Payments

- stored payment methods
- cards
- bank accounts
- payout rails
- payment vault
- transaction authorization

## Naming Guidance

Use "passes," "loyalty passes," and "digital passes" for this product. Avoid making "wallet" the product name or main category.
